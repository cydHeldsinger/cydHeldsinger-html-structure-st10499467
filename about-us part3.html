About HELDSKIN

Brief history: The company was started in January 2025 by Cyd Heldsinger to provide organic skincare at affordable prices to lower-income customers (18–30 years old).

Mission: To bring affordable organic skincare to the masses — focusing on safe, low-cost formulas for people who can't otherwise afford organic skincare.

Vision: Grow HELDSKIN to supply lower-income households across South Africa with high-quality, chemical-light, cost-effective skincare.

Team

HELDSKIN is family-run by Cyd, Lereece and Craig Heldsinger.
